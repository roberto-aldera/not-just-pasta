Peanut soba noodles

Mmmmm...

Ingredients

Main dish
  • 270g soba noodles
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • ⅔ cup chopped coriander
  • ⅔ cup shelled edamame beans
  • Sesame seeds, for sprinkling
Sauce
  • ¼ cup natural peanut butter
  • ¼ cup freshly-squeezed lime juice (we use lemon)
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp honey/maple syrup
  • 1 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • ½ tsp ground ginger

    Instructions

  1. Prepare fresh ingredients and mix.
  2. Prepare sauce (remember to heat peanut butter before adding other ingredients).
  3. Cook noodles according to packet instructions, then rinse with cold water.
  4. Add noodles and sauce to fresh ingredients and mix.
  5. Serve and sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Serves 4.
