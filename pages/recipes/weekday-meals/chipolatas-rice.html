Chipolatas and rice

Mmmmm...

Ingredients

  • 16 to 20 chipolatas (about 4/5 per person, or 150g each)
  • 2 onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 mug of rice (about 90% full)
  • Spinach (~150g)

    Instructions

  1. Slice onions lengthways and fry. Fry chipolatas up too, allowing to brown slightly.
  2. Add both tins of chopped tomatoes and leave to simmer for about 30 minutes. Add salt/pepper to taste, and enough herbs to give some character. A splosh of red wine does no harm as things continue cooking. Allow the salsa to reduce so that it thickens nicely.
  3. Cook the rice and when thatâ€™s ready, add the spinach to the salsa to wilt for a minute.

Serves 4 to 5.
