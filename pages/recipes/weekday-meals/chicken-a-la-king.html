Chicken a la king

Mmmmm...

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped into bite-sized chunks
  • 250g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 160g rice

    Instructions

  1. Fry chicken until brown. Add onion, fry until golden.
  2. Add peppers/peas and mushrooms and cook until softened.
  3. Add can of soup and simmer until chicken is cooked through.
  4. Serve on rice.

Serves 4.
