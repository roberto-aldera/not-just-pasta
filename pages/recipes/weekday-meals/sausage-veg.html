Sausage and veg roast

All in one easy dish

Ingredients

  • 8 Pork sausages (2 each, or 3...)
  • Red and yellow pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Zucchini
  • Red onion (or white/brown)
  • Baby potatoes
  • Olive oil

    Instructions

  1. Chop up veg and put everything in one dish in the oven. Easy!
  2. Serve with some wholegrain mustard for the sausages.

Serves 4.
