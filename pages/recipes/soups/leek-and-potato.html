Leek and potato soup

Veggie goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 large carrots, chopped
  • 2 sticks of celery, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400g of leeks, quartered lengthways and diced
  • 400g potatoes
  • 2 chicken stock cubes
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Mixed herbs
  1. Chop the onions, carrots, celery, leeks, and garlic. Be sure to quarter the leeks length-ways, then dice. Fry up all the veggies in oil.
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut into 1cm cubes.
  3. Make stock using about 1.8 litres of water, and add to vegetables.
  4. Then add potatoes and bring to the boil. Simmer for 10 minutes with the lid on, and season to taste.

Serves 6.
