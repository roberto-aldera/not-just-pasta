Warm grain mix

Mmmmm...

Ingredients

  • 200g farro, quinoa or pearl barley
  • 1L vegetable or chicken stock
  • 150g pancetta
  • 1 leek, cleaned and finely chopped
  • Scant 1 Tbsp of salted butter)
  • 4 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 sprig of rosemary (leaves only)
  • 2 large handfuls of baby spinach or shredded kale
  • 100g cooked chestnuts, broken up
  1. Cook grain of choice until tender, then drain.
  2. Warm the butter and oil in a pan over medium heat, then add the pancetta/bacon, leek and rosemary and cook until soft.
  3. Add the cooked grain, spinach and chestnuts to the pan, stirring to combine.
  4. Cover and cook until the leaves have wilted and the chestnuts have warmed through. Serve warm or cold.

Serves 4.
