Catalan chickpeas

Mmmmm...

Ingredients

  • 140g fresh baby spinach
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 230g dried chickpeas, soaked and cooked (or 2 cans)
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ⅓ cup pine nuts, toasted if desired
  1. Rinse spinach and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large frying pan, then add onion and sauté until soft.
  3. Add garlic, paprika and cumin and stir to combine; cook for one minute.
  4. Reduce heat to medium and add the still-wet spinach.
  5. Cover with a lid and wait until spinach has wilted.
  6. Stir in chickpeas, raisins and pine nuts, stir and cook for a few minutes until heated through.
  7. Season with 1tsp salt and black pepper to taste.

Goes well with some good bread, tomatoes, and cold meats.

Serves 4.
