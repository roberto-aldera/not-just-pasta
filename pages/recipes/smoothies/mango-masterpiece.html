Mango masterpiece

Mmmmm...

Ingredients

  • 1 large mango (or 2 smaller ones)
  • 1 banana (frozen is good, to keep things cold)
  • 2 Tbsp yoghurt
  • Squirt of honey
  • Dash of coconut
  • Sprinkle of oats
  • Milk to get it to 800ml
  • Chia seeds on top
  1. Add the things to a blender, and whoosh. Feel free to add mint for a bit of summer freshness.
  2. Pour and then sprinkle chia seeds on top (keeps them crunchy).

Serves 2.
