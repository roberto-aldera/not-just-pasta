Banana blitz

Mmmmm...

Ingredients

  • 3 bananas (overripe ones you weren't going to eat anyway)
  • 2-3 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 Tbsp yoghurt
  • 3-4 Tbsp oats (optional)
  • Milk to get it to 800ml
  • Chia seeds on top
  1. Add the things to a blender, and whoosh. You might need to add ice cubes if using fresh bananas. It's generally easier to add the frozen things last as they tend to blend better when immersed in the rest of the softer contents.
  2. Pour into two glasses, and then sprinkle chia seeds on top (keeps them crunchy).

Serves 2.
