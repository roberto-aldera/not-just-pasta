Pasta with peas

always a good idea.

Ingredients:

  • 350g pasta
  • 300g peas (frozen is fine)
  • 2 medium onions
  • 150g pancetta
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
  1. Dice up onion and fry in some olive oil, adding in pancetta once onion has browned.
  2. After 5 minutes or so, add in peas and a little water and allow to simmer (season at this point).
  3. Once pasta is cooked, add it to the peas mix and add more olive oil and black pepper to taste.

Serves 4.
